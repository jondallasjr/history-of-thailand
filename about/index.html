About This Site

Welcome to History of Thailand, a comprehensive digital resource dedicated to exploring Thailand's rich cultural heritage and historical journey from ancient kingdoms to modern times.

Our Mission

History of Thailand aims to make Thai history accessible, engaging, and accurate for students, educators, history enthusiasts, and curious learners worldwide. We believe that understanding Thailand's past is essential to appreciating its present and shaping its future.

Through carefully curated content, we showcase the achievements of Thai kingdoms, the influence of key historical figures, the evolution of cultural practices, and the major events that have shaped the nation.

Accuracy & Standards

We are committed to historical accuracy and scholarly rigor. All content on this site is:

  • Researched: Based on reputable historical sources and academic publications
  • Reviewed: Fact-checked against multiple authoritative sources
  • Cited: Includes references to original sources where applicable
  • Updated: Regularly revised as new historical research emerges

We acknowledge that history is interpreted and understood through different perspectives. Where interpretations vary among historians, we aim to present multiple viewpoints respectfully.

Our Sources

The content on this site draws from:

  • Academic historical texts and scholarly journals
  • Translations of Thai historical chronicles and documents
  • Archaeological findings and museum collections
  • Thai government historical records and archives
  • Peer-reviewed historical publications
  • Interviews with historical experts and academics

Each major article includes a sources section with references to help you explore topics further.

What You'll Find Here

Timeline

A comprehensive chronological overview of major events, kingdoms, and pivotal moments in Thai history.

Historical Periods

In-depth explorations of Thailand's major historical periods, from prehistoric times through the Chakri Dynasty.

Influential Figures

Cultural Heritage

Information about Thai traditions, art, architecture, religion, and cultural practices through the ages.

Who This Site Is For

History of Thailand serves:

  • Students: Researching Thai history for school and university projects
  • Educators: Looking for reliable teaching materials and historical context
  • History Enthusiasts: Interested in learning about Thailand's fascinating past
  • Travelers: Wanting to understand the cultural and historical context of destinations
  • Researchers: Seeking starting points for deeper historical investigation
